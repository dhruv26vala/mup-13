About Us

Welcome to the MNIT Environmental and Developmental Issues Reporting Platform, an initiative aimed at enhancing communication and promoting sustainability within our campus community. Our platform empowers students, faculty, and staff to easily report and track environmental and developmental concerns. By providing a user-friendly interface, we ensure that every member of our community can contribute to a safer, cleaner, and more vibrant campus environment. We believe that fostering transparency and accountability is essential in addressing the diverse needs of our university.

Our mission is to create a proactive approach to campus maintenance and well-being. Through features like complaint tracking, access to curated environmental articles, and data insights, we aim to not only resolve issues but also educate and engage our community in sustainability efforts. By leveraging the collective knowledge and experiences of our users, we can identify recurring challenges and work together to implement effective solutions. Join us in our commitment to building a greener, healthier MNIT for all.

What We Do:

  • Complaint Registration: Submit environmental and developmental issues easily
  • Complaint Tracking: Monitor the status of your complaints in real-time.
  • Environmental Articles: Access articles on sustainability and best practices.
  • Survey Data: View insights and trends related to campus issues
